Features

Everything You Need in a Companion App

Packed with advanced features to enhance your smartwatch experience

Smart Notifications

Receive calls, messages, and app notifications directly on your wrist. Customize which apps can notify you.

  • ✓ Call & SMS alerts
  • ✓ Social media notifications
  • ✓ Email sync
  • ✓ Custom vibration patterns
💪

Fitness Tracking

Comprehensive workout tracking with real-time metrics and detailed analytics.

  • ✓ 50+ workout modes
  • ✓ GPS route tracking
  • ✓ Calorie counter
  • ✓ VO2 Max estimation
❤️

Health Monitoring

Advanced health sensors integration for continuous wellness monitoring.

  • ✓ Heart rate monitoring
  • ✓ Blood oxygen (SpO2)
  • ✓ Sleep analysis
  • ✓ Stress level tracking
🎨

Watch Faces

Hundreds of customizable watch faces or create your own designs.

  • ✓ 500+ free watch faces
  • ✓ Custom face builder
  • ✓ Photo watches
  • ✓ Complications support

Cloud Sync

Seamlessly sync your data across devices with end-to-end encryption.

  • ✓ Automatic backup
  • ✓ Multi-device sync
  • ✓ E2E encryption
  • ✓ Export data anytime

Advanced Settings

Fine-tune every aspect of your smartwatch with powerful customization options.

  • ✓ Button remapping
  • ✓ Display settings
  • ✓ Power management
  • ✓ Developer tools
